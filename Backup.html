What Are Evolutionary Algorithms?

Evolutionary algorithms are a family of optimization techniques inspired by the grand experiment of natural selection. Think of them as a digital ecosystem where candidate solutions - individuals - compete, mutate, and recombine over generations to discover optimal or near-optimal results. This process mimics biological evolution‚Äôs power to adapt complex organisms naturally to survive and thrive.

At AGI Labs, we harness evolutionary algorithms to create AI that doesn't just learn statically, but evolves dynamically. By continually exploring countless variations of behavior and strategy, our systems discover creative solutions that escape local pitfalls. This regenerative approach fuels resilience and agility in ever-changing environments.
